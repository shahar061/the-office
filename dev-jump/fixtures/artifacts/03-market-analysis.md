# Market Analysis — Habit Streak Tracker

## Competitive landscape
- **Habitica:** gamified, multi-habit, complex.
- **Streaks (app):** closest competitor, multi-habit, iOS-only, paid.
- **Notion habit templates:** powerful but manual.

## Differentiator
Radical single-habit simplicity. Every competitor lets you track many habits; our hypothesis is that the constraint itself drives follow-through.

## Target user
Adults (25–45) who have tried habit apps before and bounced off complexity. Prefer simple utility apps.

## Pricing hypothesis
Free with optional one-time upgrade for reminder customization and export.

## Risks
- Single-habit constraint may feel too limiting for power users. Mitigation: keep it simple in v1; expand only if retention suffers.
