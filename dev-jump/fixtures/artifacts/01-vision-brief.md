# Vision Brief — Habit Streak Tracker

## Problem
People start habits with enthusiasm, then lose momentum after a few days because they lack a visible sign of progress.

## Vision
A minimal mobile app where users pick one habit, check it off each day, and watch a streak counter grow. One habit only — the simplicity is the feature.

## Success criteria
- A first-time user goes from signup to first check-in in under 30 seconds.
- Returning users complete their daily check-in in under 5 seconds.
- Users maintain their streak for at least 7 consecutive days in the first month (tracked metric).

## Non-goals
- Multiple habits per user (explicitly out of scope for v1).
- Social features, sharing, leaderboards.
- Habit suggestions or coaching.

## Open questions
- Push-notification tone and cadence (nightly reminder vs. adaptive).
- Reset policy if a day is missed (strict: streak resets; lenient: grace day).
